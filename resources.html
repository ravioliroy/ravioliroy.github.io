Resources

A list of resources people can use to study about sexual health on their own time.

The American Sexual Health Organization

This website, created by the American Sexual Health Organization (ASHA for short), gives advice on how to maintain a healthy sexual life. The info on this website covers not only how to properly prevent STDs, but sex positivity and how you can make your sex life more healthy in general.



Go Ask Alice!

Go Ask Alice! is a website run by a team situated in Columbia Universty. This website allows you to send in questions to the team regarding health topics in general, including anything you might want to know about sexual health.



Planned Parenthood: Sexual Info for Teens

This website, which was created by the Planned Parenthood organization, is a great one stop resource for teenagers to learn many things about sexual health, ranging from various questions about sex to questions about puberty and several topics inbetween. This website is not only good for teens wanting to study on their own, but can also be a great starting point for parents to use if they want to sit down and talk to their children about sex for the first time.
